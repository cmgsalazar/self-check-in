Hello, friend.

We get it. Life can sometimes feel overwhelming. You give so much of yourself to everything around you, and it’s easy to forget that you deserve care, too.

This interactive quiz is intended to help people who may be struggling to reconnect with themselves. It is designed to take a little weight off your shoulders. Set aside some time to allow yourself to work through each step; do not rush or skip ahead. No one is keeping score of how fast or slow you go.

But remember that this is not meant to replace professional and medical advice. Consider reaching out to friends, loved ones, and professionals for additional support. Even if you feel like life is going well for you, we hope you still find this a good resource. Let this be a safe space for you to treat yourself with care. We’ll sit with you. Or stand.
